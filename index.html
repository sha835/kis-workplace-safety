About the Course
Everything You Need to Get Certified
The CPO® certification is the leading credential for pool and spa operators. Our course covers it all — no fluff, no confusion.

The Certified Pool/Spa Operator® (CPO®) certification program is the world's leading education and certification program for pool and spa professionals, recognized by health departments across the country.

Our online course breaks down the material into clear, practical lessons. You'll learn water chemistry, filtration, maintenance, safety regulations, and facility management — everything you need to pass the exam and operate with confidence.

Whether you're a facility manager, property manager, hotel operator, or aquatics professional — this certification demonstrates competence and fulfills regulatory requirements.

Common Questions
The Certified Pool/Spa Operator® (CPO®) certification is the leading credential for professionals who manage and maintain pools, spas, and aquatic facilities. It's issued by the Pool & Hot Tub Alliance (PHTA) and recognized by health departments across the United States.
Anyone responsible for the operation and maintenance of a public or semi-public pool or spa. This includes facility managers, property managers, hotel and resort staff, municipal employees, HOA managers, aquatic directors, and maintenance personnel.
The course runs over two days with live online instruction via Zoom. The certification exam is administered at the end of the second day. Total time commitment is approximately 16 hours.
The CPO® certification is valid for five years from the date you pass the exam. After that, you'll need to take a renewal course to maintain your certification.
Don't worry — most students pass on their first attempt. The exam is open-book, and our course thoroughly prepares you for every topic covered. If you don't pass, we'll work with you to arrange a retake.
The CPO® certification is the most widely accepted pool operator credential in the United States. It's recognized by health departments in all 50 states. If you have specific questions about your local requirements, feel free to contact us.
